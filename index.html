Transformers: Rise of the Dark Spark

Action Game 2014

Single-player campaign level design layout.

In 2014, Hasbro contracted Edge of Reality to develop a game based on the Transformers franchise. My primary responsbility was to serve as a level design consultant for the single player campaign.

This project was a tale of caution. Tight deadlines and resources really impacted final quality of the project. What we pulled off was a miracle, regardless. I was split between two projects at the time, and was unable to really contribute beyond some layout design and technical guidance on the engine.

Aristotle

Personal Educational Project WIP 2026
Download Executable

A study for procedural world generation using Godot Engine 4. This is my current project as of January 2026, and is in active development. At this moment, it's intended to be a study into procedural world generation, but may turn into a full game project at a later time.

As more features are added, I will update the build link accordingly. It currently features cellular automata and Perlin Simplex generation.

Features on the roadmap include: sea level control, biome generation, forestry, mountain ranges, rivers, settlements, and cities. Later, local area map generation is planned.
